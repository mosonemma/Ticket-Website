About the Event

The annual Kanyangareng River Carnival is a powerful youth-centered edutainment festival held every 5th January along the river banks of Amudat. Founded in 2017, it has grown into a platform that empowers, inspires and unites young people through creativity, culture, mentorship and community engagement.

This year, we invite well-wishers and partners to join a fundraising drive to support logistics, youth development activities, educational programs and community empowerment projects.
