About the Excursion

The Creation Steppers Annual Excursion is a powerful youth experience combining adventure, networking, and entertainment. Organized in partnership with Creation Steppers Youth Development Initiative, this event empowers youth through creativity, exposure, and real-life experiences.

🏍️ Special Feature

Experience our Motorbike Adventure Ride across the scenic landscapes of Turkwel Gorge.
